About

We are a team of international students and chinese students at CQUPT from different countries but one goal "More educated world through computers"

As students and future adults, We are taking the social responsibility of making our wolrd a little "awesome". Our first undertaking is to collect second hand or new computers and donate to poor kids. We think that computers in today's world plays a major role in personal development, therefore, we want kids to start learning computers skills, internet and make the most of it.

Our Second undertaking is presenting talks shows around CQUPT with special guests, that share their experience with computers, life and how it had an impact in their social, academic, proffesional life. Our goal is to show that computer+internet are very powefull tools, if used in a productive way can make a difference in our lives.
